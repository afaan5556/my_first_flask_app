Recruiting Automation Tool

Automating notification email actions for recruiting teams

_

Overview

Recruiting teams receive multiple action-requiring automated notifications from Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) such as Greenhouse each day. They tend to take action on these notifications as they come in, often manually extracting the key data needed for each action one email at a time.

This results in a disjointed and inefficient workflow that is prone to manual error.

RAT minimizes these manual actions and the associated error margin, while also creating a new stream of candidate scheduling activity data that enables load balancing and surfaces leading forecasting indicators.

Anti inbox anxiety

The automation moves standard action-requiring emails out of the inbox to custom labels for focused "power-hour" actioning without inbox anxiety.

Scheduling power hour

The automation enables focused and contiguous actioning from consolidated key data tracker sheets instead of out of the inbox, one wordy email at a time.

Cover through visibility

The tracker sheets enable team members to easily cover for each other by providing visibility into each others candidate pipelines whenever needed.

Leading indicators

By aggregating notification email level activity, the automation surfaces candidate volume trends and load balancing opportunities across the team.
